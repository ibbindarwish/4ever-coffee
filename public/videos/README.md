## Roastery tour video

Drop your generated video here to make it appear in the "Inside Our Roastery"
section on the Roastery page (`/shop/roastery`):

- `roastery-tour.mp4` — the video itself. Muted, looping, autoplay — keep it
  short (5–15s) and silent-friendly since browsers block autoplay-with-sound.
- `roastery-tour-poster.jpg` — a still frame shown while the video loads
  (also used if the visitor's browser blocks video entirely).

Until `roastery-tour.mp4` exists, the section automatically falls back to a
branded gradient background — nothing breaks, nothing needs to change in the
code. Just add both files with these exact names and rebuild/redeploy.
