# World Cup 2026 AI Short — "Street to Stadium"

Content brief for an AI-generated YouTube Short + companion behind-the-scenes
long-form video. Not part of the live app — this is a production reference
document for planning/filming.

> **Disclosure reminder:** mark this as AI-generated/synthetic content when
> uploading (YouTube Studio → Content → Details → "Altered or synthetic
> content"). Avoid official FIFA logos, real player likeness, or the word
> "official" in titles/thumbnails — trademark risk.

---

## 1. Concept

A short (under 45s) video connecting football fans across everyday settings —
a street game, a sports bar, a rooftop gathering — that builds to a massive
stadium crowd. The idea: football as a universal, connecting force, not a
recap of any real match.

## 2. Shot list (paste one prompt per AI video generation, 5–8s each)

**Shot 1 — Street game**
> Golden hour on a quiet city side street, a group of kids playing pickup
> football, worn ball bouncing off a cracked wall, laughter, dust catching the
> low sun, handheld camera at kid's-eye height following the ball, warm
> nostalgic color grade, photorealistic, no visible logos or jersey branding,
> no readable text.

**Shot 2 — Sports bar**
> Interior of a packed neighborhood sports bar at night, dozens of people
> shoulder to shoulder, eyes fixed upward off-camera, warm neon and
> string-light glow, camera slowly pushes through the crowd toward a bright
> unseen screen, tension building, photorealistic, no visible logos, no
> readable text.

**Shot 3 — Rooftop gathering**
> Rooftop party at dusk, string lights overhead, a crowd of friends gathered
> tightly around a phone or small screen held up, sudden eruption of joy and
> jumping, confetti of hands in the air, handheld camera shake on the
> celebration beat, warm cinematic grade, photorealistic, no readable text.

**Shot 4 — Stadium reveal**
> Drone shot pulling back from a massive stadium lit up at night, thousands of
> tiny lights from phones and flags in the stands, city skyline in the
> background, epic scale, slow cinematic pull-back revealing the full bowl of
> the stadium, deep blue night sky, warm stadium glow, photorealistic, no
> visible logos or team branding.

**Shot 5 — Sync finale**
> Rapid match-cut montage: the street kids cheering, the bar crowd erupting,
> the rooftop group jumping, the stadium crowd roaring — four locations
> flashing in perfect rhythm to a beat, all sharing the same warm
> golden-hour-to-night color grade, final frame holds on the stadium crowd
> mid-roar before cutting to black.

**Consolidated single-paragraph prompt** (if a tool only accepts one prompt):
> A short cinematic film connecting football fans across a city: kids playing
> pickup football on a golden-hour street, a packed sports bar erupting at
> night, a rooftop crowd celebrating around a phone screen, and finally a
> massive lit-up stadium at night packed with fans — all tied together by
> match-cuts on the ball and on moments of cheering, consistent warm
> cinematic color grade throughout, handheld and drone camera work,
> photorealistic, no visible logos, jerseys, or readable text anywhere.

## 3. Execution notes

- Generate 2–3 versions per shot, pick the best — first attempts vary a lot.
- Cut on motion (match cuts) — e.g. ball bounce in Shot 1 lines up with the
  crowd jump in Shot 3.
- Music: AI-generated instrumental (Suno/Udio) or YouTube Audio Library
  (pre-cleared for monetization). Build to a peak on Shot 5.
- Captions: one hook line early, one payoff line in the last 2 seconds.
- Export: 1080×1920, under 45s total, `#Shorts` in the title.
